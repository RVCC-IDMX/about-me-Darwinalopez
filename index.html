I like to play Baritone horn!

This past year I was a member of the Reading Buccaneers Drum and Bugle Corps, a marching ensemble that operates and competes every weekend in the northeast area.

I intend on auditioning for a world class Drum Corps, They operate all summer so you stay with them from mid may through august. It's very physically and mentally intensive and a great opportunity to learn and to perform to tens of thousands of people in the country.

I like motorcycles,

I have owned a motorcycle for the past two year. It has become one of my favorite hobbies, it's really calming for me.

I am very interested in aviation!

I have started flight lessons before, however it was too expensive for me to continue; but that hasn't stopped me from loving aviation! I intend on completing flight training in the future!
